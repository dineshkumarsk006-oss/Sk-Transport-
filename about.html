Who We Are

Construction Logistics, Done Right.

SK Transport bridges the gap between quarry, yard, and job site. Our promise is simple: deliver the right material, in the right quantity, on time — across all of Coimbatore and surrounding districts.

With a versatile fleet of heavy-duty dump trucks, tipper trailers and flatbeds, and a dispatch team trained in construction logistics, we understand that every hour of delay at a site has a real cost. We plan accordingly.

  • Fleet: Dump trucks, tipper trailers, flatbeds — heavy duty
  • Coverage: All of Coimbatore + surrounding districts

  • Communication: ETA updates, clear dispatch coordination

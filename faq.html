Delivery & Coverage

Scheduling Your Delivery

Plan your material delivery with confidence using these answers.

Which areas in Coimbatore do you service?

+
We deliver across all of Coimbatore — Peelamedu, Singanallur, Saravanampatti, RS Puram, Gandhipuram, Hopes College, Vadavalli, Pollachi, Mettupalayam and more. Extended delivery to surrounding districts available — contact us with your site location for confirmation.

How quickly can you dispatch after a confirmed order?

+
Standard orders with 24–48 hours notice are confirmed and scheduled efficiently. For same-day urgent requirements, call dispatch directly — we'll confirm availability and aim to fulfill within the day based on fleet status.

Do you deliver to narrow lanes or restricted site access?

+
Often yes. Share your site photos and access dimensions so we can assign the right vehicle size. We operate various fleet sizes from larger tippers to smaller trucks suitable for constrained access sites.

Do you offer weekend and holiday deliveries?

+
Yes — weekend delivery is available by prior appointment. Call our dispatch team at least 24 hours in advance to book a weekend slot. Holiday delivery is subject to fleet availability.
Orders & Materials

Pricing & Products

Everything you need to plan quantities and budgets accurately.

What is the minimum order quantity?

+
Minimum order varies by material type: 1 load for sand and earth materials, 5–10 tons for aggregates and stones, and 2,000 pieces for Wirecut Bricks. Contact us for confirmation specific to your order.

What materials do you supply?

+
We stock 16+ materials including M-Sand, P-Sand, River Sand, 6MM–40MM aggregates, Blue Metal, Boulder, Solling, Sizestone, Gravel, Kuranai, Red Soil, Wirecut Bricks and Flyash. See our full catalog on the Products page.

Are your materials lab certified and IS standard?

+
Yes. Our aggregates and sand products are lab tested to IS standards (IS 383 and related specifications). Quality certification documents are available on request. We supply only consistent-grade materials across every batch.

Do your prices include delivery?

+
Our quotes are all-inclusive — material cost and delivery charges combined into one transparent price. No hidden fees. We factor in distance, vehicle type and load size when calculating delivery cost.

Do you offer bulk or project-rate discounts?

+
Yes — we offer preferential rates for high-volume and long-term project orders. Contact us with your total project volume and timeline for a custom rate proposal.
Quality & Logistics

Our Standards

How do you ensure consistent material quality across deliveries?

+
We source exclusively from certified quarries and yards. Material lots are tested before dispatch and graded consistently. Any quality concern is addressed immediately — your satisfaction is non-negotiable.

What if my delivery is delayed due to traffic or unforeseen circumstances?

+
Our dispatch team monitors active deliveries and proactively communicates any delays. You'll be notified with a revised ETA well in advance, so your site crew can plan accordingly. We don't let surprises reach you unannounced.

How do I get a quote?

+
Use our Contact page form with your material, quantity, delivery address and preferred date — we'll respond within 1 hour. Or call us directly at +91 90959 54562 for an instant verbal quote.

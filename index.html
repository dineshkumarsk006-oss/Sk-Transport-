Common Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Which areas in Coimbatore do you deliver to?

+
We cover all major areas of Coimbatore including Peelamedu, Singanallur, Saravanampatti, RS Puram, Pollachi, Mettupalayam and all surrounding districts. For extended delivery, contact us with your site location.

What is the minimum order quantity?

+
Minimum order varies by material — typically 1 load for sand and earth, 5–10 tons for aggregates, and 2,000 pieces for bricks. Contact us for confirmation and volume-based pricing.

Do you deliver on weekends?

+
Yes — weekend delivery is available by prior appointment. Call our dispatch team at least 24 hours in advance to book a weekend delivery slot.

How quickly can I get a quotation?

+
We respond to all inquiries within 1 hour during business hours (7 AM–7 PM, Mon–Sat). For urgent requirements, call us directly for immediate pricing.
